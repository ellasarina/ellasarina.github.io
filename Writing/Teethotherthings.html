Teeth & other things

Teeth

teeth want. they want to know where they are placed. they want to leave little indentations and ruddy splotches and sweet bruising. they want to spit and tear and rend. they want the click-clack of each other. teeth want to touch (though they can hardly feel)

Jaw

jaw hates. it hates and it aches.

Tongue

tongue loves. it laves. it languishes in its languages. it loves to squirm and curl. it loves whatever shape it holds.

Lips

lips are tender, despite everything.
